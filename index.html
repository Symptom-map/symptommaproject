SymptomMap

What should we call you?

This tool is designed for people who already have one or more mental health diagnoses confirmed by a professional. If you already have a diagnosis, this helps you map and understand your symptoms — it is not a tool for self-diagnosis.

How it works

1
Select your diagnoses
From the left panel. Each one you add will ask a few questions to personalize your map.
2
Generate suggestions
The AI will suggest symptoms based on your specific profile. Accept, reject, or edit each one.
3
Make it yours
Add your own symptoms, move nodes freely, and double-click any node to open its notes.
